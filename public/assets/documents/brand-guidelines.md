# LIFTS Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Logo Usage

The LIFTS logo is the primary visual identifier for our organization. Consistent usage strengthens brand recognition.

#### Primary Logo
- Use `Logo.png` for most applications
- White version for dark backgrounds
- Dark version for light backgrounds

#### Logo Clear Space
- Maintain minimum clear space equal to 20% of logo height
- Never place other elements within this boundary

#### Minimum Size
- Digital: 100px minimum width
- Print: 1 inch minimum width

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Don't change logo colors
- ❌ Don't add effects (shadows, gradients)
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds
- ❌ Don't rotate the logo

## Typography

### Primary Font
**D-DIN** - Used for headings, navigation, and emphasis

### Font Weights
- D-DIN Bold - Headings, buttons
- D-DIN Regular - Body text, navigation

### Fallback Stack
```
font-family: 'D-DIN', 'Arial', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;
```

### Text Styling
- Headings: ALL CAPS with letter-spacing
- Body: Sentence case
- Navigation: ALL CAPS

---

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional** - We're serious about aerospace
- **Innovative** - Pushing boundaries
- **Educational** - Fostering learning
- **Collaborative** - Team-focused

### Writing Guidelines
- Use clear, concise language
- Avoid jargon when possible
- Be enthusiastic but factual
- Focus on achievements and capabilities

---

## Photography Style

### Preferred Imagery
- High-contrast, dramatic lighting
- Space and aerospace themes
- Team collaboration shots
- Technical equipment close-ups
- Launch and mission events

### Image Treatment
- Convert to high-contrast when needed
- Dark overlays for text legibility
- Maintain sharp focus on subjects

## Applications

### Website
- Dark theme (black background)

- Card-based content layouts
- Smooth scroll animations

### Social Media
- Consistent logo usage
- Dark theme preferred
- High-quality mission imagery

### Print Materials
- Logo on all materials
- Consistent color palette
